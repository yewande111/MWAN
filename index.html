About Us

The Medical Women’s Association of Nigeria (MWAN) Ondo State Chapter is a branch of the Medical Women’s Association of Nigeria (MWAN), affiliated to the Nigerian Medical Association and the Medical Women’s International Association.

The Association with its motto, – ‘Matris Animo Curant’ -Healing with the love of a mother is a non-governmental, non-political and non-profit organisation that provides a forum where female medical doctors and dentists discuss and implement programmes aimed at improving health, especially of women and children.

To provide sustainable means of communication or engagement with Government, public, private, and international organizations that will lead to improvement in the implementation of healthcare delivery in Nigeria.
